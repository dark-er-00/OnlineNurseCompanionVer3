Common Health Questions & Evidence-Based Answers

This guide provides medically accurate information for common health concerns among students. This page is for educational purposes only and does not replace professional medical consultation.

Seek Immediate Medical Care If You Experience:

  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Sudden weakness or numbness on one side
  • Seizures or fainting
  • Severe allergic reaction (swelling of lips/throat)
  • Uncontrolled bleeding
  • High fever above 39°C lasting more than 3 days
